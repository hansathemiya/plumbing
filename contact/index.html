Frequently Asked Questions — Contact & Booking

What is the best way to contact SIX7 Plumbing?

For emergencies, call +94 75 824 4216 immediately. For non-urgent quotes, WhatsApp or email works best. Sending photos via WhatsApp helps us give you an instant price estimate.

Do you charge for coming to give a quote?

No. Our site visits and quotes are free. If you proceed with the repair, the visit fee is waived. If you choose not to proceed, a small diagnostic fee may apply for complex inspections.

How quickly will you reply to my message?

We aim to reply within 15 minutes during business hours (8 AM - 8 PM). After hours, our emergency team monitors messages for urgent cases only.

Can I get a quote over the phone?

Yes, for standard jobs (tap installation, leak repair). For complex renovations, we may need to see photos or visit the site to ensure accuracy.

Do you cover my suburb?

We cover all of Colombo and most Western Province suburbs. Check our Service Areas list below. If you're unsure, call us to confirm.

Is there a minimum charge for small jobs?

Yes, there is a minimum service call fee to cover travel and diagnostics. However, this is waived if you proceed with the repair work.
