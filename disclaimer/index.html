Service Disclaimer & Terms of Engagement

Last Updated: April 2026 | Applies to all service requests via website, WhatsApp, or phone.

By requesting any plumbing service from Six7 Plumbing, you acknowledge that plumbing diagnostics are inherently unpredictable. The terms below outline our pricing structure, operational boundaries, and customer responsibilities. Continuing with a booking constitutes full acceptance of these terms.

science

1. Diagnostic Nature of Plumbing Work

Plumbing systems are largely hidden behind walls, slabs, and fixtures. Initial descriptions ("simple blockage," "minor leak," etc.) are often incomplete. Our technicians diagnose on-site. What begins as a straightforward request may reveal collapsed pipes, misrouted lines, full cesspits, or prior DIY complications.

Quotes are estimates. Final billing reflects actual time, labor, equipment, and third-party services deployed to resolve the issue. We do not guarantee fixed pricing for hidden-work categories.

payments

2. Payment Policy: You Pay for What Gets Done

Our core rule:

You are responsible for all costs incurred during service, regardless of the original request. This includes:

  • Diagnostic and assessment time
  • Labor from technician arrival on-site
  • Equipment usage (jetters, cameras, pumps, etc.)
  • Third-party services (e.g., gully bowser, municipal coordination)

If a "drain unclog" escalates to cesspit emptying because waste had nowhere else to go, the bowser, labor, and hazard handling are billable. No exceptions.

3. Travel & Inspection Charges

  • Base Fee: LKR 2,500 within 5km of Mt Lavinia
  • Distance Surcharge: +LKR 450 per additional 5km block
  • Waiver Policy: The visiting fee may be waived if you proceed with a same-day repair — at Six7 Plumbing's sole discretion. This is not guaranteed, automatic, or negotiable.
circle

4. Premium & Biohazard Service Rates

Work involving wastewater, raw sewage, septic tanks, or biohazard zones requires specialized PPE, containment protocols, and mental/physical risk exposure. Rates reflect this reality:

  • Wastewater Blockages: LKR 5,400/hr (applied when standard rods/hoses fail or escalation is required)
  • Sewer / Septic / Raw Sewage: LKR 18,000 minimum engagement + LKR 7,500–9,500/hr from the 3rd hour onward
  • Water Pump Callout: LKR 5,000 (may be waived upon same-day repair, at our discretion)

warning Nobody wants to touch biohazards. We do. The premium rate covers safety, disposal, and the reality of hazardous work.

gavel "Price too high" complaints after service completion will not be entertained.

If you proceed with booking, you accept our published rates and diagnostic billing model. Post-service disputes based on personal assumptions will result in:

  • No refunds or price adjustments

If our rates don't align with your expectations, please book elsewhere. If you book with us, you accept the terms.

6. Limitation of Liability & Customer Responsibilities

Six7 Plumbing is responsible for professional diagnostic and repair work. We are not liable for:

  • Pre-existing structural damage, municipal line failures, or prior contractor errors
  • Delays caused by inaccessible work areas, locked gates, or unprovided water or power
  • Unforeseen code violations or outdated plumbing layouts not disclosed at booking

Customers must ensure safe access, accurate address and distance information, and clear communication of known issues prior to technician arrival.
