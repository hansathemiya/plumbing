60-minute guarantee for Colombo 1-10, 90 minutes for outer suburbs. Emergencies: burst pipes, flooding, sewage backup, no water, gas leaks.

Burst pipes, flooding, sewage backup, no water supply, gas leaks. Renovations and installations are not emergencies but we schedule them fast.
