Plumbing is diagnostic work. We cannot see inside your walls, under your slab, or through your pipes until we begin. What you describe as a "simple blockage" may be: collapsed pipe, misrouted waste, full cesspit, tree roots, or construction debris.

  • You pay for what gets done — not what you called for. Third-party services (gully bowser, camera) are your responsibility.
  • We do not provide fixed quotes for hidden-work categories. We assess, then bill for actual work.
  • "Price too high" complaints after work completed will not be entertained. No refund. No negotiation. Blacklisting applies.
  • Wastewater/sewage work carries inherent unpredictability. You pay for bowser, labor, hazard premium. No exceptions.

quiz Pre-Booking FAQ

Can I get a fixed price before you come?

For visible simple jobs: Yes. For blockages/leaks behind walls/sewage: No. Fixed pricing on hidden problems is irresponsible.

What if you can't fix it?

You pay for diagnostic time and attempted resolution. Assessment fees apply. We charge for professional effort.

Why is sewage work so expensive?

Biohazard work: PPE, disposal protocols, industrial equipment, and technicians willing to do what 99 percent refuse.

What if I think the price is unfair after the job?

Our rates are published. Post-service price disputes = bad-faith and will result in permanent service denial.
