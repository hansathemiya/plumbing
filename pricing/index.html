Pricing FAQs (Answering "How Much?" Searches)

How much does a plumber cost in Colombo?

Most standard repairs range LKR 2,000–15,000. Service call LKR 1,500 (waived with repair). Written quote before work — no hidden fees.

Do you charge extra for weekends or nights?

No. Same rates 24/7. Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours — neither do we.

Can I get a quote over WhatsApp?

Often yes. Send photos for a price range. Complex jobs need on-site estimate.

What if the price changes after you start?

It won't — unless hidden damage is found. We'll pause, show you, and get approval first.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For jobs over LKR 50,000: 40/40/20 installment plan.

Are your prices cheaper than agencies?

Often yes (direct work = no platform fees). Plus our Price Match Guarantee ensures fair value.
