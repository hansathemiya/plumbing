bolt What to Do RIGHT NOW (While Waiting for Us)

If you have a major leak or flood:

1

SHUT OFF THE WATER

Locate your main water valve (near meter or street entry). Turn clockwise until fully closed. Call us if you can't find it — we'll guide you.

2

TURN OFF ELECTRICITY

If water is near outlets or appliances, switch off main breaker. Don't touch panels with wet hands.

3

CONTAIN THE WATER

Use buckets, towels, or mops. Move furniture away. Place container under active drips.

4

CALL SIX7 IMMEDIATELY

+94 75 824 4216 or WhatsApp with photos. Tell us location, leak type, and if water is still flowing.

Emergency Leak Repair FAQs

How fast can you arrive for a leak emergency?

60 minutes for Colombo 1-10. 90 minutes max for outer suburbs. We dispatch nearest technician.
