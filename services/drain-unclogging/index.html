bolt What to Do RIGHT NOW (While Waiting for Us)

If your drain is completely blocked or overflowing:

1

STOP USING WATER

Don't flush toilets, run sinks, or shower. Additional water will worsen the backup.

2

CONTAIN THE OVERFLOW

Place buckets/towels under leaking fixtures. Move rugs, electronics & furniture away.

3

TRY A PLUNGER (If Safe)

Use flange plunger for toilets, cup plunger for sinks. Create tight seal, pump 10-15 times. ❌ Do NOT use chemical drain cleaners.

4
