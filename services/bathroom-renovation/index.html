Bathroom Renovation FAQs

How long does a bathroom renovation take?

Budget refresh: 1-2 days. Standard upgrade: 3-5 days. Premium remodel: 7-10 days. Written timeline provided.

Do I need to vacate my home during renovation?

Not necessarily. We contain dust/debris, but you won't have access to that bathroom during active work. Plan alternative facilities.

Can I reuse my existing tiles or fixtures?

Often yes. We'll assess during free site visit and advise on cost-saving opportunities.

What if hidden damage is found?

Common in older properties. We'll pause, show you the issue, provide written quote, and proceed only with approval.

Do you coordinate with other trades?

Yes. For standard/premium packages, we manage tilers, electricians, painters — one point of contact.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes: 90-day workmanship on plumbing, 1-year leak guarantee on waterproofing. Fixture warranties from manufacturer.
