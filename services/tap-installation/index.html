‚ö° What to Do BEFORE We Arrive (Prep Tips)

1

SHUT OFF WATER

Locate isolation valve under sink/basin; turn clockwise. If none, shut off main supply.

2

CLEAR THE AREA

Remove items from under sink/around tap. Place towel/bucket to catch residual water.

3

HAVE YOUR TAP READY

Unbox, check parts (hoses, washers). Take photos of old setup for reference.

4

CALL/WHATSAPP SIX7

DON'T

  • Attempt to force a stuck tap (may damage pipes)
  • Use excessive plumber's tape without guidance
  • Ignore a slow drip (wastes water)

DO

  • Take before photos for warranty/insurance
  • Ask about water-saving aerators
  • Request post-install pressure test
