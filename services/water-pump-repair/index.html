Water Pump Repair FAQs

Why is my water pump running but not pumping water?

Airlock, blocked foot valve, dry tank, or worn impeller. Most issues fixed within 90 minutes.

How do I know if my pump capacitor is bad?

Pump hums but won't start, trips breaker, or starts after manual spin. Capacitors cost LKR 800–2,500.

What causes low water pressure even with a working pump?

Clogged pipes, faulty pressure switch, leaking suction line, or undersized pump. We perform full pressure test.

How much does water pump repair cost in Colombo?

Most repairs range LKR 3,500–12,000. Diagnostic LKR 1,500 (waived with repair). Written quote before work.

Is it cheaper to repair or replace a water pump?

If motor burnt or pump >7 years old, replacement often better. Minor repairs save 60–80%.

Which water pump brands do you service?

All major: Grundfos, Pedrollo, Shimge, Leo, Shimizu, local brands. Common spare parts in stock.

Do you repair submersible pumps?

Yes. Specialized extraction tools available. Call for safe removal and diagnosis.

Can you install an automatic pressure controller?

Yes. Installation takes 2-3 hours. Labor from LKR 6,500 + controller cost.

How often should I service my water pump?

Every 6–12 months. Includes electrical check, impeller cleaning, pressure calibration.

Why do pumps fail more during monsoon in Colombo?

Humidity causes electrical corrosion, power fluctuations, and sediment in tanks. Ask about monsoon-prep checks.
